##  Project Overview
This repository presents my Digital Literacy Project completed as part of the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University. The objective of this project is to develop essential digital skills required for academic and professional success in today’s technology-driven world.

Through five structured tasks, I explored areas such as digital awareness, professional online presence, coding platforms, communication etiquette, and cybercrime prevention. This project reflects my learning, practical implementation, and understanding of responsible digital behavior.

##  Objectives
- To understand the concept of digital literacy
