Social Media Do's and Don'ts for Students

----------------------------------------

DO'S:
- Use strong and secure passwords for all accounts
- Keep your profile information professional and accurate
- Think carefully before posting anything online
- Respect others and maintain polite communication
- Verify information before sharing it with others

----------------------------------------

DON'TS:
- Do not share personal or sensitive information online
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Do not post offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation
- Do not use weak or common passwords

----------------------------------------

Note:
Always use social media responsibly and be aware of your digital footprint.
