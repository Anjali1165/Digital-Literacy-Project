Title: Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist

 Prevention Tips for College Students

*	Always verify the sender before clicking on any email or message link 
*	Do not click on suspicious or unknown links received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email 
*	Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts 
*	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email and banking 
*	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into personal or financial accounts 
*	Do not share personal information such as phone number, address, or passwords 
online 



 
 UPI / Financial Safety Tips 

*	Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone 
*	Do not accept unknown payment requests on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or 
Paytm 
*	Always verify the receiver's name before sending money 
*	Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources 



 
 Reporting Cybercrime

*	Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
*	Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24×7)
