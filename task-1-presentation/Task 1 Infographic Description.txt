Task 1: Infographic-Description

For the infographic, I decided to use Canva because it is 
easy to use and has a wide variety of templates for new 
users. I have designed an infographic on digital literacy 
awareness. I have focused on three major aspects: 
digital literacy, internet safety, and professional online 
presence.
 I have explained digital literacy in the infographic by 
stating that digital literacy is the power to use digital 
technology in a responsible manner. I have also 
included some key safety aspects that need to be 
considered by every internet user. I have also 
emphasized the need for maintaining a professional 
online profile, which can be useful for students on 
platforms such as LinkedIn and GitHub.
 The infographic is simple, easy to read, and visually 
appealing. I have tried to ensure that the infographic is 
visually appealing so that students can learn the key 
concepts easily. 
The challenge I faced in designing the infographic is 
choosing the exact amount of information to present. 
However, I have learned how to present information in a 
simple manner.
